Enjoy culture and history in this vibrant

Top three activities places to visit in Bogura

Visit archaeological site "Mahasthangarh"

Mahasthangarh is one of the earliest urban archaeological sites discovered thus far in Bangladesh.

Visit "Kherua mosque"

According to the Mughal architecture, their mosques consist of only a prayer hall, which is now single-aisled with three or five bays.

"Mohammad Ali Palace Museum and Park"

The museum and adjacent park has been established within the Nawab Palace on the western banks of the Karatoya River.
